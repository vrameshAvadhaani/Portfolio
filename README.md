# Competitor Analysis Template for Product Managers
Steps used in this project:<br>
1. Role definition of the PM and the outline of the organisation <br>
2. Getting metrics to evaluate your product: Understanding the product space, Evaluation Parameters <br>
3. Understanding key competitors in the current time frame <br>
4. Define your position and what should you base your roadmap for <br>
5. Make necessary artifacts : a report, and a presentation for pitching within the PM team
